About me

Hi, I am Ademola, some of my friends call me Puregem. I'm a Web Developer and Web Designer. I'm currently an undergraduate, pursuing a degree in Computer Science. I have a great interest and passion in web design, and creating stunning and effective websites. My ultimate goal is to contribute to innovations of positive change, and now, I am working towards it! I am always open to meet new people, suggestions, and exciting opportunities.

I love developing unique websites that convert visitors into customers and help your business grow. Also, I like working in a team, you'll learn faster and much more. As the saying goes: two heads are better than one.

If you have a project that needs some creative injection then that's where I come in! My job is to build your website so that it is functional and user-friendly but at the same time responsive and attractive.

When I am not coding, you will find me busy with my phone, surfing the internet or cooking.
